About Us

Our salon opened in February of 2018. Owners Kayla and McKenna have dreamed of opening there own salon for as long as they could remember. They are so excited to share their passion of hair with the Balitmore community. Kayla and McKenna went to school together and persued beauty school after learning how to run their own business in business school. Their salon specializes in baylages, highlights/foils, extensions, and traditonal cuts and colors. Their staff are all highly appraised hair stylist whom they have worked with before. Get to know our hairstylist below and book your appointment with one of them today!

Lisa Weber

Lisa is from Pennsylvania and attended The Aveda Institute and completed their cosmotology program and has been a hair stylist ever since. She currently lives in Baltimore with her boyfriend and puppy. Lisa's specialty is brunette baylages and caramel highlight colors. She loves all the brunettes out there, but is a suck for a good blonde as well!

Katrina Smith

Katrina is from Maryland and attended Empire Beauty School where she completed the cosmotology program. Katrina moved to Baltimore to do hair and has had many amazing opportunities to further her career as a hairstylist and we are so lucky she is with us here at our salon! Katrina's specialty is blondes, she loves giving a nice blonde baylage or working to keep the whole head blonde with all the highlights!
